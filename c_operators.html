Symbol Name Description Precedence Example

/ Division Divides first operand by the second 13 a / b
% Modulus Returns remainder of a division 13 a % b
= Assignment Assigns value of right operand to left operand 2 a = 10

!= Not equal to Checks if two operands are not equal 9 a != b

<< /td> Less than Checks if first operand is less than second 10 a < b

>> Right shift Shifts bits of the operand to the right 12 a >> 1

sizeof Size of Returns the size (in bytes) of its operand 15 sizeof(a)
, Comma Separates expressions, evaluates them left to right 1 a = 1, b = 2

-= Subtract and assign Subtracts right operand from the left and assigns result 2 a -= 5
*= Multiply and assign Multiplies left operand by the right and assigns result 2 a *= 5

%= Modulus and assign Takes modulus using both operands and assigns result 2 a %= 5

|= Bitwise OR and assign Performs bitwise OR and assigns result 2 a |= b
^= Bitwise XOR and assign Performs bitwise XOR and assigns result 2 a ^= b
