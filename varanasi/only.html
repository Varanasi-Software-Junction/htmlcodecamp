Dashashwamedh Ghat

One of the oldest and most significant ghats in Varanasi, known for its evening aarti.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most famous Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the heart of Varanasi.

Sarnath

A major Buddhist pilgrimage site where Gautama Buddha is said to have given his first sermon.

Assi Ghat

One of the oldest ghats in Varanasi, known for its scenic beauty and tranquility.

Ramnagar Fort

A fortification in Varanasi near the Ganges River, known for its museum and scenic views.

Manikarnika Ghat

A famous cremation ghat on the banks of the Ganges, central to Hindu funeral rites.

Bharat Mata Temple

A unique temple dedicated to Mother India, featuring a relief map of the country carved in marble.

Tulsi Manas Temple

A modern temple dedicated to Lord Rama, known for its beautiful murals depicting scenes from the Ramayana.
